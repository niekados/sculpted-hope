Discover the Beauty of
Handmade Pottery!

Hey, there!

I'm Viltė, the heart and hands behind the cozy corner of handmade pottery you've stumbled upon. Picture this: a snug little studio in Vilnius, where every clay creation is infused with warmth and crafted with a sprinkle of magic.

My journey with pottery started as a therapeutic dance with clay that quickly became my happy place. There's something incredibly special about the way your hands mold raw clay into something both functional and beautiful. It's like creating a piece of soulful art that's meant to be a part of your daily life.

Beyond being an artist, I'm thrilled to share this journey with you as I transition from hobbyist to a small business owner. My studio is not just a workspace; it's a warm embrace, a space where you're invited to witness the creative process firsthand. Each piece you discover is more than just a product; it's a slice of my heart, carefully shaped into something I hope brings joy and comfort to your world.

Whether you're a fellow pottery enthusiast, a seeker of cozy vibes, or someone yearning to add a touch of handmade charm to your space – welcome! Come explore the nooks and crannies of my ceramics, where every piece is a personal invitation to slow down, savor the moment, and surround yourself with the tangible beauty of handmade art. Can't wait for you to join me in this journey of crafting coziness and spreading a little extra warmth through the art.

With love, Viltė.
